# Structure

1. answer a question, don't write about a topic
2. give a one sentence answer
3. expand that thesis into a paragraph outline
   - 5–8 sentences
   - Each sentence = one major supporting claim
   - This becomes your paragraph map.
4. expand on each sentence into it's own paragraph. Each paragraph requires the following
   - State a claim
   - Provide evidence
   - Explain the relevance
   - Tie back to the thesis
5. iteratively refine each paraphraph, sentence and argument
